About Us

Every person at Social WiFi believes in businesses delivering amazing customer service. This is why we build technology that makes a customer or guests experience, even better. We want a business to carry the journey online and after they leave the premises.

Why

We believed that technology could help and that guest WiFi was a starting point for staying in contact with customers. Back in 2013, we realised that businesses that deliver their services offline are at a massive disadvantage to those that sell online. They do not know who their customers are and therefore have absolutely no way to encourage them to come back once they’ve left. And this is why Social WiFi was born.
We are not a data capture company (ok, we do capture data), but the cool stuff – the magic – comes from the tools that we build to help businesses engage with their customers and guests.
Businesses that use Social WiFi have more customers coming back more regularly and attract new business because of the reviews that we gather for them.
